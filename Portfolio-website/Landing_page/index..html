About Me

How it Started

My journey into tech hasn't been a straight line. I started exploring UI/UX design because i was drawn to how websites look and feel, but i soon realized i wanted more than just designing layouts. I wanted to build, to see ideas come to the life through code.

Finding my Direction

At first i wasn't consistent. I would practice once in a while, sometimes just opening my phone to write little code and feel like i achieved something. For a long time, i felt like i didn't have a clear direction and found myself jumping between different interest without fully committing to one.

But at some point, i made a decision to stop spreading my focus and take one thing seriously and give it real effort. I choose to commit to front-end development, and i started learning through platforms like freecodeCamp, practicing consistently, and repeating concepts until i understood them better. Along the way, i kept exploring and improving, even when things felt confusing.

Where i Am Now

Recently, i was accepted into the Shecode Africa mentorship program, which i'm about to begin. I see it as an opportunity to gain more exposure, learn from others, and continue developing my skills. At tis point, i see this journey as something i'm building for myself, step by step, with goal of becoming more confident and creating websites i'm truly proud of.

Right now, i'm still in the learning stage, focusing on improving my skills and understanding how everything works. I've built simple project like login page, nenu page, to-do lists, and blog layouts, and i'm continuing to grow everyday.I haven't gotten into Javascript yet, but i'm working towards it and i'm excited for what's ahead.
